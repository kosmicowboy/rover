        Description

        Kaffeemeister Coffee Bar and Drive-Thru is a quick stop for espresso also featuring free wi-fi, a large dog-friendly garden seating area, pastries, and light fare! Bring Fido along and try a Hammerhead espresso or Caffe au Lait, or snack on an Acai Bowl or Cinnamon Rolls. Local musicians are featured on Friday nights! Kaffeemeister Coffee Bar and Drive-Thru is open in El Cajon Monday through Saturday from 7:00am to 5:00pm and Sunday from 7:00am to 4:00pm. The drive-through opens at 6:00am Monday through Saturday.
